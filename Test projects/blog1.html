Gerlie's Cafeteria


Was built in November 1999

When Gerlie Tolentino got separated from her husband. She got the idea of selling breakfast to her grandmother in Manila. She first started selling Champorado and spaghetti in front of their house. The first renovation of Gerlie’s Cafeteria was on February 2002, then started selling fried rice.

I went to travel with my squad in Angeles City and after a long walk we felt hungry and then we saw this cafeteria, offers great budget breakfast meals. We went inside and we tried their best sellers. I tried the Champorado and it taste so good that I want to eat here everyday. All of us was blown away for the taste of the food because we aren't expecting that it was so good for the price itself.
